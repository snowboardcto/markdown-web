Powder Day Gear Guide

Same powder-day.md — rendered warm & readable, the way you like it.

Riding deep snow is its own discipline. This guide covers the essential setup for a backcountry powder day — what to bring, what to leave, and the one thing you should never forget.

Avalanche safety first. No line is worth your life — check the forecast, carry the gear, never ride alone.

The checklist

  • Beacon, shovel, probe
  • Powder board or stiffer all-mountain deck
  • Spare goggles — fogging ends days early

Board sizing

Float comes from surface area, so size up in powder — roughly 3–5 cm over your all-mountain length, more if you ride steep and deep.
